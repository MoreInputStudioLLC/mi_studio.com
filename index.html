Letters & Liches - Array

Part word game, part dungeon crawl. Spell or be eaten!

Build words from your letter rack to strike down monsters and descend the depths of the dungeon. A time sink that's pure joy — and it'll sharpen your ability to find words in scrambled letters while it happily devours your evening.

Who we are

More Input Studio

More Input Studio came screaming into the world as an accidental LLC — born of a personal coding project on one bored evening, when the mood called for something casual but challenging. That little project grew into Letters & Liches - Array: a glorious time sink that also happens to sharpen your unscrambling skills.

The team

We're a couple of lifelong gamers who figured it'd be fun to put something of our own into the world. Heather is a data analyst in insurance who started her tech journey writing HTML back when the internet still ran on dialup. Joshua is a software guy — and a bartender of over a decade — whose very first college job was at the campus computer help desk. We met a long, long time ago and have collected our share of odd “couple's hobbies” along the way. Before this one, it was making bean-to-bar chocolate.
